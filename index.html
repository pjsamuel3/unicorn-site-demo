About the Author

Meet Annabel

Annabel Schiøtz lives in Norway with her husband, her eight grandchildren nearby, and one very fluffy dog. On her walks through the woods she photographs the troll-like trees and bushes lurking between the pines — and those discoveries have turned into a beloved series of picture books.

Her stories are about the adventures of a grandmother called Besti, her dog Lula, seven colourful trolls, an unhappy red dragon, a rainbow-coloured duck, and various other characters. The grandchildren, of course, wouldn't be left out.

All books are available in both English and Norwegian.
