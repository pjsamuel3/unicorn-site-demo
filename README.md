# Annabel Schiøtz — Author Fan Page

A small fan-made website celebrating the children's books of **Annabel Schiøtz**, a Norwegian author who writes magical stories about colourful trolls, rainbow ducks, friendly dragons, and five-horned manycorns.

## Live Site

👉 **[pjsamuel3.github.io/unicorn-site-demo](https://pjsamuel3.github.io/unicorn-site-demo/)**

## About the Author

Annabel lives in Norway with her husband and writes stories inspired by the troll-like trees and bushes she photographs on her walks through the woods. The stories feature a grandmother called Besti, her dog Lula, seven colourful trolls, and her grandchildren. All books are available in English and Norwegian.
